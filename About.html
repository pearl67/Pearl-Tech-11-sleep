My Story

My story is that I feel for me personally that I get a better more relaxed sleep if I am listening to music and I know that is the same with a lot of the population of the world. I have done my research and I have found that there are no websites like the one I I have created. All the websites I have found all have just YouTube videos connected to them. I have created an all new one where you can put on a 3 hour soundtrack turn off your phone and have a relaxing night sleep without having to worry about a YouTube add waking you up.

Our Mission

To help you get the best sleep that you deserve.
